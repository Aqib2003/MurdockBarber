Our History

Murdock Barber Shop . is a classic upscale barbershop. We offer a variety of services, catering to everyone. Hounds is a gentleman's shop, reminiscent of the classic barbershop. We are striving to bring the memories back, with solely the clientele in mind. People pay for quality - Hounds is where you will get it. We are barbers who love our craft, providing top notch service that helps men Stay Sharp.
